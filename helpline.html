There are many people suffered from mental illness so if you encounter one in Malaysia please contact:

HEAL Line 15555

HEAL Line 15555 is Malaysia's National Mental Health Crisis Helpline, established to provide immediate emotional and psychosocial support to individuals facing mental health challenges. Operating daily from 8:00 AM to midnight, including public holidays, the helpline is managed by professional counseling officers from the Ministry of Health Malaysia.

Befrienders Kuala Lumpur

Offers 24/7 confidential emotional support for individuals facing distress, despair, or suicidal thoughts.

Mental Illness Awareness and Support Association (MIASA)

Provides counseling, assessments, support groups, and various programs to educate and support individuals with mental health challenges.

Malaysian Mental Health Association (MMHA)

Offers support via phone for mental health issues, with qualified professionals providing psychological support services. Financial subsidies are available to ensure access to necessary therapy and support.

Talian Kasih

A 24-hour helpline providing assistance for various crises, including domestic violence, child abuse, and mental health issues.
