Depression


What's Depression

Depression is characterized by persistent felling of sadness, hopelessness, and a lack of interest or pleasure in activities. it can affect how you feel, think, and behave, leading to a range of emotional and phisical problems.and reuire a long lasting professional intervention.


Depression in the world

Study said over 300 people are estimated to suffer from major depressive disorder, equivalent to 4.4%of the world population.

symtom of Depression

In general the symtom of depression include:

  • Low Energy
  • Feeling Very Sad
  • Hopeless
  • Thoughts Of Self-Harm
  • Suiside
  • Change In Eating Behavior
  • Sleep Changes
  • Loss Of Interest In Hobbies And Aktivities
  • Diffulty Concentrating Or Making Decision

What Cause Depression

  1. Biology: brain chemical imbalances,genetic,or health issues.
  2. Psychology: trauma, stress or negative thinking
  3. Environment: isolation, financial problems, or major life changes
  4. Lifestyle: poor sleep substance use, or unhealthy diet.

Often, these factors combine to trigger depression


How To Reduce It

  1. Talk to someone: share your fellings with a friend, family member, or therepist.
  2. Get moving: exercise regularly, even just a short walk.
  3. Sleep well: aim for 7 to 9 hours of good sleep
  4. Eat healthy: focus on nutritious foods and aviod too much sugar or alcohol.
  5. Do things you enjoy: even small activities like listening to music or reading would help
